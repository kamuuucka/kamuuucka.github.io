About Me

My name is Kamila and I am an aspiring Technical Designer.
I specialise in creating various tools in Unity.

My passion lies in making other developer's lives easier. In addition to tool development, I often come up with ideas for enhancing game mechanics or bringing even the most unconventional concepts of Game Designers to life.
Currently, I am in my final year of the Creative Media and Game Technologies program at Saxion University of Applied Sciences. Alongside my studies, I work as a developer at Serious XR, where I focus on creating products that have a real-world impact.

Download Page

You know how difficult it is to catch up with the Internet? How easily you can get lost and get from a video of a cute cat to the whole theory about how pyramids were built by the aliens? In our game you can experience it.

Together with your friend run through the 3 layers of the Internet: surface web, deep web and dark web. Help each other to get the highest score possible. Be careful on the crossroads! The anonymous users might try to lead you to the "worse" parts of the online world.

This project was made for an Overkill Festival 2023 in Enschede. The topic was "the outburst of digital swamp".
