Swamp Unleashed

My Role:

Programming Lead | Technical Designer

Download Page

You know how difficult it is to catch up with the Internet? How easily you can get lost and get from a video of a cute cat to the whole theory about how pyramids were built by the aliens? In our game you can experience it.

Together with your friend run through the 3 layers of the Internet: surface web, deep web and dark web. Help each other to get the highest score possible. Be careful on the crossroads! The anonymous users might try to lead you to the "worse" parts of the online world.

This project was made for an Overkill Festival 2023 in Enschede. The topic was "the outburst of digital swamp".

collaboration and communication | project ideation and concept development | client interaction | procedural generation | custom editor scripting | tool development | optimisation and performance management

Buggle

My Role:

Programmer | Technical Designer

Download Page

Sometimes browsers are bugged. Pictures won't open, pages won't refresh, and links won't work. What if instead of this, your browser got infected by actual bugs? After downloading Buggle (not the most secure browser), you have to deal with a lot of bugs.

In Buggle you play as its typical user - an insect enthusiast who wants to learn about their favourite creatures. Unfortunately, your peaceful browsing gets disturbed by ants and cockroaches, whose only goal is to make your experience miserable.

Project was a submition to XP x LINK Gamejam 2023 and won the award for best theme interpretation.

Skills learned:

time management | project ideation and concept development | creative thinking | user's input interaction | creating small but engaging product

StepUp Fred

My Role:

Programming Lead | Technical Designer

Download Page

Learning English does not need to be boring! Help Fred (a little, friendly animal) to rebuilt his house by finding the correct words in the big letter grid.

StepUp Fred is a two player game. One person reads the definitions of the words, and the other person needs to find them in the letter grid. But be careful! Grid contains words that are there just to confuse you.

Escape Baba Yaga

My Role:

Programming Lead

Download Page

Baba Yaga is an enigmatic or ambiguous character from Slavic folklore. In some motifs she is described as a repulsive or ferocious-looking old woman who fries and eats children. In Bulgarian, baba means 'grandmother' or 'old woman'.

Tow players play as two children who got kidnapped by Baba Yaga. They have to escpae her puzzle-filled house by working together. In this interactive escape room you will find yourself immersed in a Slavic/Balkan environment and, hopefully, escape the Baba Yaga.

The game requires a laptop and two phones to connect. All the devices need to be connected to the same WiFi network.

Skills learned:

client-server connection | cinemachine cameras
